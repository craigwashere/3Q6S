Boxes, cabinets, shelves or desks with components, office supplies, equipment, or tools are properly labelled and their content is consistent with their description.  						
Stocked items are kept with the indication such as part number, name and quantity.						
Items are managed to be returned in their designated locations after use.						
All chemicals are properly marked and kept in designated areas						
Line zoning is according to standard (line width, color) and not damaged						
Items which are frequently used are arranged in an ergonomic way						
Items inside desks and storage drawers are sorted and organized.	
Desk chair should be kept under the desk during non-working hours.						
Worktables and shelves are lined up straight, and no items are kept directly on the floor.
